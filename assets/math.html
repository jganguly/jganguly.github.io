Engineering Mathematics
Mathematics is a fundamental tool used by engineers and scientists to help understand various phenomena. In the the book below, I introduce various concepts in mathematics, from basic to advanced, that are explained in a crisp and concise way. The book leverages SciPy to derive the solutions through symbolic computing. chatGPT is used to generate the scipy code listing.

Tensor Calculus and Differential Geometry - by Jaideep Ganguly
This book is an attempt to provide a compact explanation of the fundamental results in tensor theory and its application in differential geometry, engineering analysis and relativity. The motivation to write this book came from the desire to develop a comprehensive content that is necessary to provide a rigorous exposure to the theory of tensor calculus so that it can effectively applied in engineering and physics.
