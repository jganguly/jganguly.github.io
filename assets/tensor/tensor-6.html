It should be noted that the components of a tensor in the new variables, are linear combinations of the components in the fold variables Consequently, if all the components a tensor vanish in any particular coordinate system, then they vanish in all coordinate systems. Or, if the components are zero at some particular point, then they are zero at this same point for all coordinate systems. This is an extremely important property of tensors.
