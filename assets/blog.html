AGI has reached a stage where it can demonstrate common sense.

GPT 4 has been released and the results have been simply stunning. Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) seems to be on steroids. OpenAI has not published the architecture and is unlikely to do so and that makes sense. It is not about stacking more transformers and tweaking the attention model, a lot of more substantive changes seem to have been incorporated to get over an order-of-magnitude improvement in its performance.

Elon Musk and others have written an open letter calling for a moratorium on AGI research citing genuine concerns but it is unclear if that's going to happen. We stand at the edge of fundamental changes in our society brought on by the spectacular advances of AGI which is likely to completely overshadow the collective intelligence of the entire human race in under a decade. This is already impacting the workforce.

I wonder if GPT 4 could tweak its own transformer layers and observe its results and see if they are getting better. That would make it self-adaptive and follow Darwin's theory of natural selection. GPT 5 will be better than GPT 4 and so on. We could end up with a species of GPTs who mix and match their architecture, somewhat like organic reproduction, and follow the doctrine of the survival of the fittest. After a series of interactions, does the state of its network mimic "consciousness"? Will it develop something similar to a "conscience"? Just as an airplane can fly, albeit very differently from a bird, we do agree on their flight capabilities. A similar construct may be applied to "conscience" and "consciousness" when it comes to GPT.

GPTs can interface with robots and impart AGI. The consequences are immense and can be scary. Just as nuclear technology has proliferated and that is a worry, over time AGI technology will also spread but keeping it closed will delay it. A regulatory framework, and oversight from qualified and trusted "observers" is needed.

We are at the dawn of augmenting our cognitive abilities with AGI and that has been possible with modern transformers. One can be optimistic that this immense power can be used to help humans lead happy and creative life. But the research will be limited to a few organizations given the immense computing power required to train the network.

GPT 4.0 has been released, it is multi modal, i.e., it accepts image and text as inputs and exhibits human-level performance in critical benchmark studies at top 10%.

It is quite extraordinary that the revolutionary self-attention mechanism with Reinforcement Learning From Human Feedback has made it so exceptional! Does the Attention Model in GPT mimic the human brain? May be to some extent, but certainly not entirely. In any case, it is impossible to answer this precisely as much is unknown about the human brain. And it does not really matter as it is getting the job done. “The question of whether a computer can think is no more interesting than the question of whether a submarine can swim” as Edsger W. Dijkstra had once said.

In the last century, with the mechanical advantage, humans exceeded the strength of animals. With tha advent of power stearing and automatic gears, every man or woman can became equally adept at driving. Now we are in the era when the human brain can be augmented by GPT to enhance their capabilities. The human brain, after over 200,000 years of evolution, has probably reached its steady state and any augmentation of cognitive abillity has to be be achieved by GPT type of technologies.

There are concerns, of course. GPT's response is based on what it has been trained with. The world is full of prejudice, bias and plain lies. GPT "learns" from documents which could be malicious and there lies a problem. Until AI figures out how to model "conscience", there can be no guard rails. Words such as "consience", "consciousness" are not quantifiably understood. Conscience is a moral sense of right or wrong. But what is right, and what is wrong? Consciousness is awareness of your unique thoughts, memories, feelings, sensations, and environments. Essentially, your consciousness is your awareness of yourself and the world around you. This awareness is subjective and unique to you. We don't know how to model these and, even if we could, each GPT model could end up have its own unique consience and consciousness. And finally, the carbon footprint for training the model is signifcant. The power required to train a single model is in excess of what 100s of US homes would require in a year.
